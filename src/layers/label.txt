LABEL MODE
s from Normal, Ctrl+s from Ins
Type labels to select target, then:
Space         Click
Fn+Space      Cmd+Click
Shift+Space   Shift+Click
Enter         Right-click
Fn+Enter      Double-click
Shift+Enter   Cmd+Shift+Click
Up            Hover (no click)
right_ctrl    Cancel

Returns to origin layer (Normal or Ins)
