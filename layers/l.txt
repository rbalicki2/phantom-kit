L Layer (l from Normal)

H = plus, Fn+H = Cmd+plus
N = equals, Fn+N = Cmd+equals
Ctrl+H = Close (Cmd+W)
Enter = Cmd+Enter

Sub-layers (press, then a-z sends modifier+letter):
Y = Cmd, Ctrl+Y = Ctrl+Cmd
U = Ctrl, Ctrl+U = Ctrl+Alt
I = Alt, Ctrl+I = Alt+Cmd
O = Cmd+Ctrl+Alt
Fn+key = modifier+mirrored LHS letter
