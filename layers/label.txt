LABEL - Homerow Click Mode
M from Normal, Ctrl+M from Ins
Type labels to select target, then:
Space         Click
Shift+Space   Right-click
Up            Hover (move mouse, no click)
Enter         Double-click
Shift+Enter   Shift+Click (extend selection)
Fn+Space      Cmd+Click
Fn+Enter      Cmd+Shift+Click
right_ctrl    Cancel

Returns to origin layer (Normal or Ins)
