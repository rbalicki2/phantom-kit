GRID MODE
Fn+M from Normal
Navigate grid (u,i,o,j,k,l,m,comma,period), then:
Space         Click
Fn+Space      Cmd+Click
Shift+Space   Shift+Click
Enter         Right-click
Fn+Enter      Double-click
Shift+Enter   Cmd+Shift+Click
Up            Hover (no click)
N             Normal mode (fine IJKL)
right_ctrl    Cancel

Returns to Normal layer
