Fn+M from Normal

Space         Click
Shift+Space   Right-click
Up            Hover (move mouse, no click)
Enter         Double-click
Shift+Enter   Shift+Click
Fn+Space      Cmd+Click
Fn+Enter      Cmd+Shift+Click
right_ctrl    Cancel

Returns to Normal layer
